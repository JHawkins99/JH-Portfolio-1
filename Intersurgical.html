Intersurgical Ltd. - Wokingham, UK

Design Engineer - 2018-2021

Whilst working at Intersurgical my design projects included:

  • Reusable air filter cartridges and sealed masks for COVID19 during pandemic
  • Connectors for breathable tubing systems.
  • Improvements to ET tubes to reduce patient trauma.
  • Used SiemensNX to create 3D part and component models, and 2D design drawings including mould information such as split lines, injection points and GD&T.
  • Held design reviews for new products including tool design using production methods: injection, extrusion and blow moulding.
  • Oversaw material and production changes to meet new standards, improving supply chain stability and efficiency.
  • Led projects from concept to customer, working alongside clinical testing teams for component validation plans, tooling methods for manufacturing optimisation, usability for client side usage and marketing in order to ensure continuity of vision.
  • Calculated and maintained budgets based on labour, tooling, research and other costs.
  • Assisted departments with legacy drawing updates and modernisation, using tooling drawings and models to include all relevant manufacturing information.
  • Ran CFD simulations to adjust high precision breathing systems and valves that would be difficult to measure.
  • Worked to ensure all products and projects comply with international BS EN ISO Standards such as 14001 as well as internal risk management and protocols, to make sure designs met high standards for the sector.
  • Completed tolerance analysis of new and existing parts to guarantee desired form, fit and function.
